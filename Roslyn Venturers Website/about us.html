Roslyn Venturers

Roslyn Venturers is a group of teenagers from 14 years to 19 years who get together and do stuff like tramping and camping. We meet every Tuesdays from 7pm to 9pm at the Roslyn Scout Hall. We have 4 leaders Sean Laing, Evan Rees, Stuart Eaton, and Allan Nisbet. Venturers develop their own programmes - from vocational activities developing self-confidence, to leadership courses and team-building exercises.
