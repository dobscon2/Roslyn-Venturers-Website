How do I join?

Come on in and join us every Tuesdays from 7pm to 9pm at the Roslyn Scout Group. You can find more information about Scouting at scouts.org.nz

Award Scheme

Venturers can work to gain the highest award in Scouting which is called the Queen Scout Award. You can earn this award by completing the Venturers Award Scheme like helping out your community, completing courses, and organize activities. The Queen Scout Award marks the pinnacle of effort and achievement and it is presented at the Government House. To learn more about the scouts Award Scheme check out scouts.org.nz
